Out-of-Town Services

Professional representation and execution beyond your immediate location, handled with the same discretion and reporting standards as all Kiuki services.

Scope of Services

  • Professional handling of out-of-town errands and assignments
  • Secure collection and delivery of documents or parcels
  • Liaison with offices, institutions, and service providers
  • On-site representation and follow-ups as required
  • End-of-day or end-of-assignment reporting

Service Terms

  • All travel, accommodation, and logistical costs are covered by the client
  • Service fees are based on task complexity and duration unless covered by a tier
  • All engagements are scheduled by prior arrangement
